ABOUT CAT ROSE

Hello! I'm Cat!

I am a multimedia artist and psychology major at Miami University in Ohio. While I am working my way towards a professional role in the field of psychology, art has always been an integral part of my life. I began drawing before I gained the ability to store memory, and have stuck with some form of creative prospect ever since. Today, I am to explore different forms and techniques and the interplay between them.


So far, I have geared my focus towards:

  • Mixed Media 2D art
  • Magazine layout art
  • 3D modeling and environment construction
  • Editorial photography modeling
  • Metalsmithing / Jewelry Design
  • Clothing design
